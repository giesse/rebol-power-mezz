Top FAQ of the Week
Looking at one of the top Frequently Asked Questions to give you the answers you need and help make your messaging experience a pleasant one.

Question: What is my Client ID?

Answer:
Only Clickatell Central accounts have Client ID's. You will have such an account only if you are using an API product (for developers).

The Client ID is typically a 6 character code (3 letters, 3 numbers) and was emailed to your registered email address on registration of your account. If you have a Clickatell Central account, but do not remember your Client ID, please contact our support team.

A Communicator account will not have a Client ID - you can login
without a Client ID.

Messenger Pro users also do not have have a Client ID - you can login normally. To automatically visit this page, click on the 'buy credits' button on the 'Options' tab of Messenger Pro.

If this does not resolve the problem you experience, please contact the support team.

 

In order to receive optimal support from Clickatell, please ensure that your details are up to date. Should you not wish to receive this weekly balance update, please change your account preferences while logged in to Clickatell Central.
